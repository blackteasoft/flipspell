FlipSpell Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Blackteasoft ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FlipSpell mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Summary: FlipSpell collects only anonymous usage data to improve your experience. We do NOT collect any personal information that can identify you.

Information We Collect

We collect the following anonymous data to improve app performance and user experience:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app (screens viewed, features used, game statistics)
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app crashes to help us fix bugs

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other personal files
  • Payment information (handled securely by Apple)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect anonymous data:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): Helps us understand how users interact with our app
    Firebase Analytics Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google): Helps us identify and fix app crashes
    Firebase Crashlytics Terms

In-App Purchases

FlipSpell offers in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Retention

Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 14 months to analyze app performance trends. Crash reports are retained for 90 days.

Children's Privacy

FlipSpell does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to use.

Your Rights

Since we only collect anonymous data that cannot identify you, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your game progress is stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
