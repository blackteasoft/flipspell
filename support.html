Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play FlipSpell?

Tap the FLIP button to reveal 15 letter cards. The game automatically detects English words along 9 different lines. Find exact matches for higher scores, and anagram matches also count!

How does the energy system work?

You start with batteries that provide energy. Each FLIP uses 1 energy. When you run out, wait for batteries to recharge (30 minutes each) or purchase more in the Shop.

How is scoring calculated?

Each letter has a point value similar to Scrabble. Rare letters (Q, Z, X, J) score higher. Finding multiple words gives combo bonuses, and discovering rare words earns multiplier rewards!

What are word rarities?

Words are ranked by how common they are: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. Rarer words give bonus multipliers to your score.

Do I need an internet connection?

No! FlipSpell works completely offline. Play anytime, anywhere.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Shop and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Your previous purchases will be restored automatically.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at blackteasoft@gmail.com with details about the issue and your device model. Screenshots are helpful!
